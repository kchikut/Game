A platform of simple and interesting games

On this site, a simple game "Miner" has been implemented, which you can play in the "Game" tab. You can familiarize yourself with the rules of the game in the "Rules" tab. Have a good game.

Rules of game

The flat playing field is divided into adjacent cells, some of which are "mined"; the number of “mined” cells is known. The goal of the game is to open all cells that do not contain a mine. The player opens cells, being careful not to open a cell with a mine. Having opened a cell with a mine, he loses. If there is no mine under an open cell, then a number appears in it showing how many cells adjacent to the one just opened are “mined”; Using these numbers, the player tries to calculate the location of the mines, but sometimes even in the middle and at the end of the game, some cells still have to be opened at random. . By opening all the “unmined” cells, the player wins.
